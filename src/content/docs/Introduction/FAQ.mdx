## What is CodePup AI?
CodePup AI is an intelligent platform that turns natural language prompts into full, production-ready websites using modern frameworks like React and Tailwind.

---

## How does the credit system work?
Each generation or deployment consumes a small number of credits.  
You can top up anytime, and paid users receive monthly credit refreshes.

---

## Can I connect my own domain?
Yes! CodePup supports custom domains on all paid plans.  
Once your site is live, just copy the DNS values we provide into your registrar settings.

---

## Can I export my code?
Absolutely. You can export clean, editable React + Tailwind code and host it anywhere — Vercel, Netlify, or your own servers.

---

## Does CodePup host my websites?
Yes, we offer instant hosting for generated projects.  
You can also integrate with third-party hosting providers if you prefer.

---

## Do I need coding experience?
Not at all. CodePup is built for everyone — founders, students, and developers alike.  
You describe what you want, and it builds it. Developers can fine-tune the generated code anytime.

---

## What technologies does CodePup use?
CodePup leverages Node.js, React, Tailwind, and AWS for infrastructure.  
The AI pipeline uses modern LLMs and code-generation techniques for accuracy and scalability.

---

## How can I contact support?
You can reach out anytime via your dashboard or at **support@codepup.ai**.
