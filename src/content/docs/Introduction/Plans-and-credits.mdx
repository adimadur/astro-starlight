CodePup AI offers flexible pricing options designed for creators, startups, and teams of all sizes. Credits are used to generate and deploy projects — simple, transparent, and predictable.

## Credit System

Each site generation or deployment consumes credits.  
Credits can be refilled anytime from your dashboard.

| Action | Credit Usage |
|--------|---------------|
| Generate new website | 15 credits |
| Modify website | 5 credits |
| Deploy to CodePup Hosting | Free (Public) |
| Export project (ZIP) | Free |

You can also earn free credits via referrals and early access programs.

---

## Billing and Subscription

Payments are securely processed via Stripe.  
All plans are billed monthly, and you can cancel anytime — no hidden fees.

Your credit balance resets at the start of each billing cycle.  
Unused credits roll over automatically for paid users.

---

## Upgrading or Downgrading

- You can upgrade instantly from your dashboard.  
- Downgrades take effect in the next billing cycle.  
- Your projects remain safe and accessible even if you pause your subscription.

---

## Refund Policy

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee if you're unsatisfied for any reason.  
Simply contact support with your transaction details.
